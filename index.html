Nikolai Tesla

Man who Spread the Light.

Tesla's Life:

  • Born On: 10 July 1856 Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia)
  • Citizenship Austrian (1856–1891) American (1891–1943)
  • Project: Alternating current high-voltage, high-frequency power experiments
  • Education: Graz University of Technology (dropped out)
  • Discipline: Electrical engineering, Mechanical engineering
  • Died: 7 January 1943 (aged 86) New York City, United States
  • Resting place: Nikola Tesla Museum, Belgrade, Serbia

"Einstein was once asked how it felt to be the smartest man alive, then Einstein replied "I don't know you would have to ask Nikolai Tesla.""
